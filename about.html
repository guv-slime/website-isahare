About Emory

My name is Emory Hare, and I am a digital artist out of Eastern North Carolina. Previously focused on pen and paper, I've now taken up digital art as my primary medium, and I'm really enjoying it so far. My style has been described as "cartoony realism," and I'm heavily influenced by cartoons like: The Ren & Stimpy Show and Spongebob Squarepants.

My art often has funny and punny elements, usually with some unknown creature as a subject. I have been doing studies of real-life creatures to bring some greater accuracy to my work, so some pieces are more realistic than others. But I know that while my general style will always be the same, I will continue growing as an artist and producing different types of art over time.
Thank you for being part of my journey and checking out my first website!
